Department of Theatre & Dance Website

College of Wooster - Department of Theatre & Dance Website

Content Strategy / Art Direction / UX / Art Direction / Development

During an exploratory meeting, it came up multiple times that the client wanted the idea and feeling of the theatre to be evident in the visual vernacular the website. The client also express the importance of seeing the quality work coming out of the department. The header image on each page will load a random picture within a set of selected images. This will change on each different page load. When a user hovers over the image slideshow controls then appear, thus giving the user another place to explore the visuals coming out of the department.

This was a collaborative project with Jake Kellogg.
