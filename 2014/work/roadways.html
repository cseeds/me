Roadway Intersections

Personal Project

I have always been fascinated by maps. I love to see the different winding roads and patterns. This personal project is an ongoing projec where I am scouring much of the US and looking for interesting intersections of highways. After going through much of the Eastern US, I started to see various patterns in the intersections.

What you are looking at here are the some of outliers of those patterns. These intersections are unique. There is a certain beauty in the flow and rhythm of the roadways.
