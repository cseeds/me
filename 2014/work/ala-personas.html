ALA Persona Videos

Art Direction / Filmography / Editing

For a bi-annual trade-show, the design team was tasked with coming up with an engaging visual that summed up the offerings of Findaway World. At the point of this trade-show, Findaway World was showing off four products. Each of these products have their own distinct users, but there are overlaps in many places. For each product there was a table and vertical TV. Each TV had a looping visual of potential users of the product below. The video shows how users interact with audiobooks during the duration of their listening.
