About

I design impactful experiences across screens and paper. I'm currently designing for a multi-screen world at Findaway.

Design, for me, is more than any one medium. Pixels, ink, wireframes, prototypes; design can be the solution to a problem. In order to reach that solution, you have to have a process. This process begins at researching and wholeheartedly understanding the content and users. This is how I begin all projects. The journey is as important as the final artifact.

Back when Google Reader announced it was shutting down, I had the oppertunity to work with Feedly over the course of a weekend. Read about it.
